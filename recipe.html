Chocolate Pancakes

These fluffy chocolate pancakes are easy to make and perfect for breakfast. They are rich, delicious, and ready in just a few minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, cocoa powder, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the egg and milk, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip the pancake and cook for another minute.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy.
